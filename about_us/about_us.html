Who Are We

ARKEYTEC is a computational design firm that offers various services like Architecture, Interior and Landscape design to clients.

We are technology enthusiasts at heart. We work with technologies like Genetic Algorithms, Parametricism, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning, Building information modeling in our project to generate better and efficient designs for our projects as well as those we provide consultations for.





Ideology

At ARKEYTEC we believe that designs should be intelligent, and a good design is produced from careful study and research, combined with technical knowledge and artistic judgment. Attention to detail, proportions, and scale, together with common sense ensure the finished product fully develops the potential within the concept.

Why technology

While we are strong advocates for technology, we don't use technology just for the sake of it. There are multiple reasons for which we use technology for:-

a) Automation

One of the things that technology assists us with is the automation of design, drawing productions, modeling, rendering, and execution. There are several aspects of technology that we use like genetic algorithms, parametric design, BIM modeling, visual scripting, artificial intelligence & machine learning, data science, drone mapping, and robotic fabrication. Design for everyone
As architects and designers, we pledge about protecting our environment, doing good for the society, helping people through design, but the reality is that architects and designers cater to only a small section of affluent people. In such a scenario, how are we contributing to the betterment of society?

How architects have tried to resolve this issue is by creating one or two designs for the unit and replicating/stamping it horizontally or vertically. Here the individual needs of the end-user are not met. Same design for everyone.

In an automated system, an architect or designer sets a larger logic. And based on the individual need of the people, site condition, local by-laws, and climatic condition user-specific design are generated.

Automation helped to design for everyone at a huge scale, according to their specific needs, site condition, local climate and local by-laws at a fraction of costs. This makes design accessible for all.

Better Design
While designing architects or designers cater to only a few aspects of design. The reason for that is the complexity involved in the design. We often see great architects having a particular style. This style emerges out of human limitations of not being able to use more parameters. The process gets further complicated when one has to design in a given period. Automation enables us to consider multiple parameters at a time. A typical artificial algorithm considers around 80-100 parameters at a time.

Time
Time is money. Designers can save lots of time by automating redundant activities and day-to-day recurring activities by letting machines and software do them for you. The time saved can be used for more productive design processes which in turn improves the quality of design.

b) Data-driven design

Design-driven design of increasing importance as it helps to achieve goals of the economy, sustainability and site conditions from a very early stage in the design process.

c) Optimization

Today’s challenges in the architectural industry are centered around energy saving, cost-effectiveness, and improving safety and occupant comfort. To address so many different challenges computational optimization is a must. To analyze and solve more and more complex design problems, we use Artificial Intelligence, Evolutionary Algorithms, Swarm Intelligence, Artificial Neural Networks, and other biologically inspired techniques for discovering high-performing design solutions.

The ability of these algorithms to solve multiple objectives very quickly make them of high importance in design optimization.

Some of the things that we optimization works great for are solar, thermal, moisture, acoustics, wind and air, energy, daylight availability, circulation, layouts, views, structure, general shape for building code/regulations, aesthetic, etc.
